We are made of

Education is not received. It is achieved. - Albert Einstein

However unlike the days of Einstein, today the plethora of careers, degrees, universities, mode of learning, countries and the locations within each country and so on makes the process of achieving it highly complex. Just to elucidate, in Jan 2015, the CSIC (Spanish National Research Council) had estimated that there were more than 23887 Higher Education Institutions (colleges and universities) across the world (http://www.webometrics.info/en/node/54). Similarly more than 1800 college majors have been reviewed and profiled by the web company MyMajors (http://www.mymajors.com/college-majors/).

Furthermore complicating the process of achieving the right fit education, the admissions process adopted by the highly selective top ranked universities has become more challenging. No more it’s just the academic grades that matter. Admissions committee closely scrutinizes the student's academic choice of courses and the scores, performance at various standardized tests, involvement in sports, clubs and extracurricular activities, and more importantly the X-factor – academic curiosity.

In this mind-boggling market of global education, EduZen firmly believes that there is no short-cuts to any place worth going. With over decades of experience the team at Eduzen helps you in DISCOVERing yourself and in the process make such highly selective top ranked universities DISCOVER you. We not only guide you through your application process but also assist you in improvizing your acceptance chances. We are known not only for assisting students with admission offers but also for obtaining financial aid – either in the form of scholarships or assistantship.

EduZen is committed to lead you to your right destination. At the end of the day, it’s all about finding the right fit and being in the right place.
